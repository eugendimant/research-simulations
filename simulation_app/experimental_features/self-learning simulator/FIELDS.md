# Social science fields coverage (v0.16)

This project supports a cross-field taxonomy for:
- behavioral economics
- social psychology
- political science
- marketing
- management / org behavior
- consumer research
- sociology
- criminology
- public policy
- behavioral finance
- health behavior
- education research
- environmental behavior
- communication / media studies
- human-computer interaction (behavioral)

In code, fields are represented as tags on Topics and Insight Units.
