================================================================================
STEP 4: THEORY-GROUNDED PERSONA LIBRARY
================================================================================

Following Manning & Horton (2025), defined behavioral personas:

PERSONA              | WEIGHT | CHARACTERISTICS
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Tech-optimist        | 25%    | High AI attitudes (5-6), moderate-high
                     |        | ownership, comfortable with AI recommendations
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Tech-skeptic         | 20%    | Low AI attitudes (1-3), reduced ownership
                     |        | in AI conditions, prefers human recs
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Hedonic-focused      | 20%    | Higher ownership for hedonic products,
                     |        | pleasure-seeking orientation
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Utilitarian-focused  | 20%    | Higher ownership for utilitarian products,
                     |        | practical orientation
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Balanced/neutral     | 10%    | Middle-range responses on all measures
---------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------
Random responder     |  5%    | Inconsistent patterns, may fail checks

TRAIT PARAMETERS (drawn per participant):
- TECH_AFFINITY: Uniform(0.2, 0.9) - shapes AI attitudes
- OWNERSHIP_TENDENCY: Uniform(0.3, 0.8) - shapes psychological ownership
- ATTENTION_LEVEL: Uniform(0.85, 0.98) - probability of passing checks

================================================================================
STEP 5: RESPONSE GENERATION RULES
================================================================================

5.1 Demographics:
    - Age: Normal(35, 12), truncated to [18, 65]
    - Gender: 50% Male, 48% Female, 1% Non-binary, 1% Prefer not to say

5.2 Manipulation Check (AI_Mentioned_Check):
    - AI conditions: 95% answer "Yes" (correct), 5% "No" (inattentive)
    - No AI conditions: 95% answer "No" (correct), 5% "Yes" (inattentive)

5.3 Psychological Ownership (5 items):
    - Base score = OWNERSHIP_TENDENCY * 5 + 1
    - Add Gaussian noise (SD = 0.8)
    - AI conditions may reduce ownership for tech-skeptics (-0.3 modifier)
    - Item 5 reverse-scored: 7 - base_score

5.4 AI Attitudes (5 items):
    - Base score = TECH_AFFINITY * 5 + 1
    - Within-person consistency (small noise SD = 0.7)
    - Item 5 (Threat) reversed: (1 - TECH_AFFINITY) * 5 + 1

5.5 Hedonic/Utilitarian Rating:
    - Utilitarian products (Dove): Mean = 2.5 (slightly utilitarian)
    - Hedonic products (Snickers): Mean = 5.5 (slightly-mostly hedonic)
    - Persona adjustment: +/- 0.5 based on hedonic/utilitarian focus

5.6 Spot-the-AI Task:
    - Random 50/50 (Picture 1 or Picture 2) - realistic guessing

5.7 WTP Comprehension Check:
    - 87% answer "Participant 2" (correct - second highest WTP)
    - 13% random incorrect answer

5.8 Willingness to Pay:
    - Base = 5 + (OWNERSHIP_TENDENCY * 3) - (PRICE_SENSITIVITY * 2)
    - Add Gaussian noise (SD = 1.5)
    - Range: 0-10 (integer)

5.9 Task Summary:
    - 20 unique phrasings randomly assigned
    - Diverse one-sentence descriptions
